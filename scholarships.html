Scholarships & Financial Aid

AWKUM is committed to ensuring no student is denied education due to financial constraints.

HEC Need-Based Scholarship

For underprivileged students. Covers full tuition fee and stipend.

Eligibility: Family income less than PKR 45,000/month.

KPEF Scholarship

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Education Foundation scholarship for meritorious students.

Eligibility: Top 3 position holders in their respective districts.

Sibling Concession

50% tuition fee waiver for the second and subsequent siblings studying at AWKUM simultaneously.

Merit Scholarship

Awarded to semester GPA toppers (3.5+ CGPA) in each department.
